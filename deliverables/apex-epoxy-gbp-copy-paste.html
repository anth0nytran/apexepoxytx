Q&A Section

Go to your Google Business Profile listing on Google Maps (search your business name). Scroll down to "Questions & answers" and click "Ask a question." Post each question from a personal Google account, then answer it from your business account.

Why do this? Google indexes these Q&As. When someone searches "how long does epoxy flooring last in Houston" — your answer can show up. This is free SEO content on your profile.
Question
How long does epoxy flooring last?
Answer
Our epoxy floor coating systems last 15-20+ years with proper care. We use 100% solids industrial-grade epoxy (not the watered-down kits from hardware stores) sealed with a UV-stable polyaspartic top coat for maximum durability. Every installation comes with a 5-year written warranty covering peeling, delamination, and hot tire pickup.
Question
How much does epoxy garage flooring cost?
Answer
A typical 2-car garage epoxy floor coating runs between $1,800-$3,500 depending on the size, condition of the concrete, and finish style (flake, metallic, or solid color). We provide 100% free on-site estimates with no obligation — we'll measure your space and give you a transparent quote. Call us at (713) 201-4005 to schedule yours.
Question
How long does it take to install an epoxy garage floor?
Answer
Most residential garage floors are completed in just 1-2 days. Larger commercial projects typically take 2-3 days. Our process includes professional surface preparation (diamond grinding or nano chemical primer), epoxy application, flake or metallic broadcast, and a UV-stable polyaspartic top coat to seal everything in.
Question
Is epoxy flooring slippery when wet?
Answer
Not with our system. We add anti-slip aggregate directly into our polyaspartic top coat, which provides excellent traction even when the floor is wet. This is especially important for garage floors where water, oil, and other liquids are common. Safety is built into every floor we install.
Question
What areas do you serve?
Answer
We serve Cypress, Houston, Katy, The Woodlands, Spring, Tomball, Sugar Land, Pearland, League City, Humble, Kingwood, Conroe, Richmond, Atascocita, and all surrounding areas in Greater Houston. We provide free on-site estimates throughout our entire service area. Give us a call at (713) 201-4005!
Question
What's the difference between epoxy and polyaspartic coatings?
Answer
Great question! Epoxy is our base coat — it bonds to the concrete and provides the strength and thickness of the system. Polyaspartic is our top coat — it's UV-stable (won't yellow in sunlight), cures faster than epoxy, and adds the glossy protective finish. We use both together as a system for maximum durability and longevity. This combination is what separates a professional installation from DIY kits.
Question
Do you do commercial epoxy flooring?
Answer
Yes! We install commercial and industrial floor coatings for warehouses, showrooms, restaurants, retail spaces, auto shops, and more. Our commercial systems are designed to withstand forklifts, heavy machinery, chemicals, and high foot traffic. We also offer OSHA-compliant safety striping. Contact us for a free commercial estimate.
Question
Can you coat outdoor patios and pool decks?
Answer
Absolutely! We use UV-stable polyaspartic coatings specifically designed for outdoor use. They resist fading, cracking, and delamination from Texas heat and weather. We add anti-slip texture for wet areas like pool decks and walkways. Our outdoor coatings are built to handle Houston-area weather year-round.
Question
What kind of warranty do you offer?
Answer
Every residential installation comes with a 5-year written warranty covering peeling, delamination, and hot tire pickup. If there's ever an issue, we come back and fix it — no questions asked. We're owner-operated (Beau & Haden are on every job), so our reputation is directly tied to the quality of every floor we install.
Question
Why should I hire a professional instead of using a DIY epoxy kit?
Answer
DIY kits from hardware stores use water-based epoxy that typically lasts 1-3 years before peeling. We use 100% solids industrial-grade epoxy — the same products used in commercial and industrial applications. The biggest difference is surface preparation: we diamond grind or use nano chemical primer to create a proper bond, while DIY kits only recommend acid etching (which doesn't work nearly as well). Our floors last 15-20+ years with a 5-year warranty. The DIY kit has no warranty and often costs more in the long run when you have to redo it.
