What You Need to Do

The optimization we've done builds the foundation — but your ongoing actions are what will actually move you up the rankings. These are the habits that separate businesses that rank from those that don't.

Most Important Get More Reviews

Ask Every Single Customer for a Google Review

This is the #1 thing that will move the needle. You have 1 review. Your closest competitor has 53. The top competitor has 320+. Every new review is a direct ranking signal to Google.

  • After every job: Text or email your Google review link (see QR code below)
  • In person: "We'd really appreciate a Google review — it helps other homeowners find us. Can I text you the link?"
  • Target: 4–8 new reviews per month minimum
  • Timing: Ask the same day the job is done, while they're still excited about their new floor
  • Make it easy: Text them the link directly — one tap to leave a review
  • Milestone targets: 25 reviews by month 3, 50 by month 6, 100 by year 1

High Impact Post Regularly

Post to Google at Least Once a Week

Google Posts show up directly on your profile and signal that your business is active. Every competitor posts regularly — you need to as well. You can do this right from the Google Business Profile app on your phone.

  • Easiest method: Take a photo of a completed project, write 2-3 sentences about it, hit post
  • Include your location: "Just finished this garage in Katy" or "Beautiful metallic floor for a Cypress homeowner"
  • Add a call-to-action: "Call for a free estimate" or "Book your consultation today"
  • Mix it up: Project showcases, before/afters, tips, seasonal offers
  • It doesn't have to be perfect: A quick phone photo with a real caption beats no post at all

High Impact Upload Photos Consistently

Take Photos of Every Project — Upload 5-10 Per Month

GarageExperts has 981+ photos. Platinum Floor Coatings has 100+. Photos drive engagement and Google tracks your photo count vs. competitors.

  • Before & after shots of every floor (this is your best content)
  • In-progress shots showing the team at work
  • Close-up detail shots of metallic swirls, flake patterns, glossy finishes
  • Team photos — people trust faces
  • Variety matters: Different angles, different project types, residential and commercial
  • Target: 50+ photos on your profile within 3 months

Ongoing Respond to Every Review

Reply Within 24 Hours — Every Single Time

Google tracks your response rate and response time. Every top competitor responds to reviews. Use the templates we provided, and always mention the type of work and location naturally.

  • Example: "Thank you! It was a pleasure installing your new epoxy garage floor in Katy. We love how the metallic finish turned out — enjoy your new space!"
  • For negative reviews: Stay professional, offer to resolve offline, never argue publicly
  • Even a short response counts — Google cares that you respond, not how long it is

Ongoing Keep Your Profile Updated

Monthly Maintenance (10 minutes)
  • Add any new services you start offering
  • Update hours for holidays or seasonal changes
  • Check that your phone, website, and address info are still accurate
  • Check your GBP Insights to see if impressions are increasing
  • Link any new social media accounts
